YOUR DATA, YOUR RULES

Three ways to keep
your history safe.

StrideTrack is local-first by default. Your data lives on your phone – we never see it unless you want us to.

01
📱

Local Storage

By default, every walk is saved directly on your phone. No account needed. Works 100% offline. Your data never leaves your device.

Default – always on
02
💾

Local Backup File

Don't want the cloud? Export your entire walking history as a JSON backup file at any time. Restore it just as easily. Your phone, your archive.

Opt-in · No account
03
☁️

Cloud Sync via Supabase

Connect a profile and your walks are automatically backed up in the cloud. Protected by PostgreSQL Row-Level Security – only your account can ever read your data.

Opt-in · Encrypted
Powered by Supabase
🔒

StrideTrack never sells your data. Ever. Cloud sync is completely optional and protected by database-level Row-Level Security (RLS). You can delete your account and all its data at any time from within the app.
