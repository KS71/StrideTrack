Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 24, 2026

StrideTrack ("we", "our", or "us") is built with a 100% focus on user privacy and data ownership. This Privacy Policy describes how your personal and fitness data is collected, used, and protected when you use the StrideTrack mobile application (the "App") and our companion website.

🔒 Local-First by Default

StrideTrack is designed to be offline-first. By default, all walking logs, goals, and app settings are saved locally on your own device. We do not track you, and we do not have access to your local data.

1. Data We Collect and How We Use It

A. Local Fitness & Walk Data

You can manually log your walks (distance and date) and set goals within the App. This data remains on your physical device. We use this data solely to calculate your progress and display statistics inside the App.

B. Optional Cloud Sync Data (Supabase)

If you choose to opt-in and sign up for our cloud backup service (powered by Supabase):

  • We collect your email address to create and authenticate your account.
  • Your walking logs, goals, and settings are uploaded and securely stored in our cloud database to allow backup and sync across devices.
  • Protection: This database is protected using PostgreSQL Row-Level Security (RLS). Only your authenticated account has permissions to read, write, or delete your data.

2. Google Health Connect Integration

StrideTrack supports integration with Google Health Connect to automatically sync walk logs and distance data from your smartwatches and other fitness trackers.

  • Data Accessed: If granted permission, StrideTrack reads your Distance and Exercise Sessions (walking and hiking workouts).
  • Purpose: We access this data solely to import your walk distances into the app, update your goals automatically, and prevent double-counting between your phone's background sensors and smartwatch GPS logs.
  • Storage: Synced walks are stored locally on your device (and optionally backed up to your Supabase account if cloud sync is enabled). We do not store or transmit this data to any other third parties.

⚡ Limited Use Policy Disclosure

StrideTrack's use of information received from Google Health Connect will adhere to the Google Health Connect Permissions Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. We do not sell, share, or transfer your Health Connect data to any advertising networks, data brokers, or third-party service providers.

3. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal or health data to outside parties. Your data is yours. The only third-party service integrated is Supabase, which is used strictly as a secure host for your optional cloud sync backups, and only when you explicitly sign up for a cloud account.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Local Data: All walking data is stored locally on your device. You can export a full backup of your data at any time via Settings → Local Backup. To permanently erase all local data, walks, goals, and settings from your device, use the Settings → Danger Zone → Delete All Data button. You will be asked to confirm twice before anything is deleted. Uninstalling the app will also permanently delete all local data.
  • Cloud Data: If you use Supabase cloud sync, your data is tied to your account. You can log out at any time from the Settings menu. To permanently delete your cloud data, please contact us and we will remove your account and all associated data immediately.

5. Children's Privacy

StrideTrack does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document.
